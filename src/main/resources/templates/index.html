"In the world of information technology, there are numerous tools and technologies that help ensure the quality of software development. In the realm of manual QA (manual testing), tools such as Jira, TestRail, HP Quality Center are often used, along with test case methods and functionality checks performed manually. Back-end development entails the use of programming languages like Java, Python, PHP, along with frameworks like Django, Spring Boot for building the server-side of applications and working with databases. Databases play a crucial role in storing and managing data.
